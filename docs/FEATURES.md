# Features Guide

## Core Features

### 🎤 Voice Practice
Record and practice speaking English with real-time feedback powered by AI.

**Features:**
- High-quality audio recording
- Real-time speech-to-text transcription using OpenAI Whisper
- Instant AI-powered feedback
- No time limits - practice at your own pace
- Audio playback for review

**How to Use:**
1. Navigate to the Practice tab
2. Tap the microphone button to start recording
3. Speak naturally and clearly
4. Tap the stop button when done
5. Wait for AI analysis
6. View your personalized feedback

### 📊 Comprehensive Feedback

Get detailed analysis in 5 key areas:

#### 1. Grammar Analysis
- Identifies grammatical errors
- Provides corrections with explanations
- Shows the corrected version
- Grammar score (0-100)

#### 2. American English Version
- Rewrites your sentence as a native American would say it
- Explains why it sounds more natural
- Suggests better vocabulary/expressions
- Naturalness score

#### 3. Pronunciation Feedback
- Identifies pronunciation issues
- Focuses on challenging sounds (R, TH, V/W, etc.)
- Provides phonetic guidance
- Highlights focus areas for improvement

#### 4. Fluency Score
- Measures your speaking pace and smoothness
- Evaluates sentence structure
- Considers pauses and rhythm
- Fluency score (0-100)

#### 5. Additional Metrics
- Vocabulary usage score
- Confidence level
- Accent detection

## Performance Metrics Explained

### Grammar Score (0-100)
Evaluates the correctness of your sentence structure, verb tenses, subject-verb agreement, and proper use of articles.

**Scoring:**
- 90-100: Excellent grammar, minimal errors
- 75-89: Good grammar, few errors
- 60-74: Acceptable grammar, several errors
- Below 60: Needs improvement

### Fluency Score (0-100)
Measures how smoothly and naturally you speak, including pacing, rhythm, and sentence flow.

**Scoring:**
- 90-100: Excellent fluency, native-like speech
- 75-89: Good fluency, occasional hesitations
- 60-74: Acceptable fluency, some hesitations
- Below 60: Needs significant improvement

### Vocabulary Score (0-100)
Assesses the range and appropriateness of words used in your speech.

**Scoring:**
- 90-100: Excellent variety, sophisticated vocabulary
- 75-89: Good variety, appropriate words
- 60-74: Basic vocabulary, adequate
- Below 60: Limited vocabulary range

### Confidence Score (0-100)
Evaluates your speaking confidence based on voice tone, pace, and stress patterns.

**Scoring:**
- 90-100: Very confident, clear delivery
- 75-89: Confident, mostly clear
- 60-74: Moderate confidence
- Below 60: Low confidence, hesitant

### Accent Score (0-100)
Measures your pronunciation accuracy with American English standards.

**Scoring:**
- 90-100: Near-native accent
- 75-89: Clear and understandable
- 60-74: Mostly understandable, accent noticeable
- Below 60: Pronunciation needs work

## Tips for Best Results

### Recording Tips
1. Find a quiet environment
2. Speak clearly and naturally
3. Don't rush your words
4. Use complete sentences
5. Speak at your normal pace

### Practice Tips
1. Practice daily for best results
2. Start with easier topics
3. Gradually increase difficulty
4. Review your feedback carefully
5. Try to implement suggestions in the next session

### Using Feedback
1. Read the corrected version carefully
2. Understand why changes were made
3. Pay attention to pronunciation tips
4. Practice difficult sounds separately
5. Re-record the same topic to compare

## Keyboard Shortcuts (Web Version)

- `Space`: Start/Stop recording
- `Enter`: Submit session
- `Esc`: Cancel session
- `R`: Repeat last recording
- `H`: View history

## Troubleshooting

### Recording Not Working
- Check microphone permissions
- Ensure microphone is connected
- Restart the app
- Check device microphone in settings

### Transcription Errors
- Speak more clearly
- Reduce background noise
- Ensure minimum 10 seconds of speech
- Check internet connection

### Feedback Not Loading
- Check your internet connection
- Wait a moment and try again
- Check if API quota is exceeded
- Review error message for details
